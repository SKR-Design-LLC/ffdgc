Introducing - The Feast  

November 16 2019

What was once known as the Harvest,  our yearly doubles event focused on fun and value, is now The Feast.

This event will be held at Phantom Falls and Bucksnort disc golf courses,  1 round at each. 
RPM Discs will be providing us with tournament stamped Ruru's and Piwak's for each player.

Zoka's Restaurant and Bar will be catering for players.

Trophies (For more than just the best M/M team) will be handled by Malachi Cowan's The Other Glass

There will also be a thermos included in each players pack, cause thats how we roll.

Special thanks to Jose Barrientos for the amazing artwork and King's Valley Wine & Spirits for their support.

 

Registration information and cost will be available in the near future.
