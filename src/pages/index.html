THE BLOOM 2019
aka Platte Canyon School District Fundraiser
July 6th, 2019

  • Presented by Foothill Flyers Disc Golf Club and BOERA (Bailey Outdoor Education Recreation Area)
  • Sanctioned C-Tier
  • Bailey, CO

Registration is live

The Foothill Flyers Disc Golf Club and BOERA are excited to bring you the 4th annual PCSD Disc Golf Fundraiser. This will be a sanctioned C-Tier event held at 1 course, Bailey DGC, located at 599 Rosalie Rd. Bailey, CO. The purpose of this event is to raise money for PCSD for all they do in maintaining and allowing us all to play Bailey DGC almost year round. We have some exciting projects planned for the course this year following up on the huge success of the DiscGolfPark turf pads installed in 2018. UPDATE (6/5) We will have brand new DiscGolfPark baskets installed in time for this tournament!!! Addition info on the Events page
