The Board

The FFDGC Board consists of a group of people that are excited to give back to the disc golf scene in the area and are committed to growing the sport.  All members contribute on a voluntary basis.  Reach out to an officer if you are interested in getting involved.

Foothill Flyers Disc Golf Club (FFDGC) is dedicated to providing quality disc golf courses, events, instruction, and introduction of the great game of disc golf to the Colorado communities along the Highway 285 Corridor; Conifer, Bailey, Pine, and Evergreen.
