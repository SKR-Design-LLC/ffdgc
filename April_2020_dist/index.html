Announcements

Flyers!
Welcome to 2020!  I'm proud to share that the website is completely up to date, the Tags schedule for the next 3 months are available, and the dates for all our our larger events are up as well! Things are good!

The "Top 5" tags list is also up to date,  but will need help keeping it that way.  If you hear of a change please let me know

Going forward into this fresh season I am really excited to share that The Pollination will be on May 2nd at Bear mountain!  This is typically our unsanctioned / fundraiser event focused on having a great time and this year should be no different. 

This new season has a not so fresh Sunday tags Ace pool sitting at just over $1250,  If we reach $1500 before an ace is hit we'll start increasing the $500 cap for a hit ace.  Also regarding Sunday tags,  we've already sold nearly 100 in the first couple matches! 

Thank you to everyone for all of your continued support,  We are looking forward to a great year of disc golf on the 285!
